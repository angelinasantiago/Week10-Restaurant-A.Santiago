ABOUT THE COCINA


Since 1960

ABUELA'S COCINA was inspired by our family's cookbook of recipes passed down from generations. Abuela was a kind and gentle soul who put so much love and care into the food she served to family, friends, and even strangers. Therefore, here at ABUELA's COCINA we want to replicate and continue that kindness and heart she had through serving quality food. We even donate food that does not get served to local food pantry's and homeless shelters around the city to ensure that as many people as possible don't go hungry. We are a restaurant dedicated to making authentic and quality Puerto Rican food. Inspired by recipes and ethically sourced ingredients, we pride ourselves on providing our visitors with quality home cooked style food.
